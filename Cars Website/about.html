HISTORY

2014

The interior of the Mercedes-AMG GT was previewed on 16 April. The car made its debut on 9 September and was officially unveiled to the public in October 2014.

1989

Between 1989 and 2002, Nissan produced its Skyline coupe. This car proved to be emblematic of Nissan and achieved great fame and success on the road, track and motorsports.

2014

The BMW M4 was unveiled in January 2014 at the Detroit Auto Show in the United States. The first generation of the BMW M4, internally called the F82, is powered by a 3-liter twin-turbo.

2019

On December 4, 2015, Porsche announced that a production car would be created. On June 8, 2018, Porsche announced that the production model will be called the Porsche Taycan. On September 4, 2019, it was presented to the public.
